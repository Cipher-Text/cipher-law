About Our Chamber

Rahman & Associates Chamber is one of Bangladesh's most respected legal chambers, founded by Advocate MD. Rahman in 2010. Located at the Supreme Court Bar Association, our chamber has grown into a comprehensive legal practice serving diverse clientele across Bangladesh and internationally.

Led by experienced advocates, our chamber houses a team of senior advocates, junior advocates, pupils, and legal researchers dedicated to providing exceptional legal representation. We specialize in Supreme Court litigation, corporate advisory, and complex legal matters requiring strategic expertise.
