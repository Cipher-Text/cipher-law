About Rahman & Associates Legal

Founded in 2005, Rahman & Associates Legal has emerged as Bangladesh's premier full-service law firm, serving a diverse portfolio of multinational corporations, financial institutions, and high-profile clients across domestic and international jurisdictions.

Our firm operates through specialized practice groups, each led by senior partners with deep expertise in their respective fields. We pride ourselves on delivering strategic, commercially-minded legal solutions that drive business success.
